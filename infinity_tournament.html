Free Fire Pro Tips

Aim Training

Practice headshots in training mode daily. Adjust sensitivity settings to find what works best for you.

Map Knowledge

Learn popular landing spots, loot locations, and strategic positions on all maps.

Squad Communication

Coordinate with your team, share resources, and develop strategies before matches.

Loot Management

Prioritize weapons, armor, and medkits. Don't overcrowd your inventory with unnecessary items.

Zone Awareness

Always keep an eye on the shrinking zone and plan your movement accordingly.

Character Combinations

Choose character abilities that complement each other in your squad for maximum effectiveness.
